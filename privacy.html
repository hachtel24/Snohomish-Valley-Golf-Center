Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data safety are fundamental to the trust you place in our family-owned business. Review our data practices below.

Information We Collect

The Snohomish Valley Golf Center website may collect both personally identifiable and non-personally identifiable information during your visit to help improve our services.

1. Personally Identifiable Information

We only collect personally identifiable details that you voluntarily submit to us, such as when filling out our event inquiry form, contact form, or membership inquiry. This may include:

  • First and Last Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Event details and dates

2. Non-Personally Identifiable Information

Our website utilizes cookies and analytical tracking files to collect standard network session logs. This includes:

  • Device Category (mobile, tablet, desktop) and Operating System
  • Browser Type and IP Address
  • Usage metrics, including pages viewed, time spent on-page, and exit URLs
  • Preferred Language settings and approximate geographical location

How Information Is Used

Collected details are utilized to fulfill your service requests and deliver customer support, specifically for:

  • Processing lesson reservations, gift card inquiries, and event rental bookings.
  • Sending facility policy updates, event notifications, or PGA clinic schedules.
  • Responding directly to questions sent through our digital forms.
  • Managing shopping cart actions or tracking abandoned checkout requests to send reminder SMS alerts.

Business Partners & Third Parties

We do not share your text messaging (SMS) opt-in data or consent status with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes unrelated to the delivery of the messaging campaign itself.

Consent and personal data are only shared with service providers supporting our direct messaging platform channels (such as telecom aggregators, telephone service providers, and vendors delivering the SMS updates on our behalf).

Cookie Policy

Cookies may be used to store preferences, monitor site engagement, and customize navigation flows during future visits. You may adjust your browser options to reject cookies, though doing so might affect specific interactive aspects of the site.
