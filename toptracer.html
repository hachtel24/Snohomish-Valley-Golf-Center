Toptracer Technology

It's Not Just Hitting Balls

Toptracer Range technology is included with every bucket of balls. Discover a fully interactive, statistics-driven golf experience.

In-Stall Screen Monitor

Immediate Value & Shot Insights

With Toptracer Range technology, every golfer—from absolute beginner to seasoned touring professional—has access to valuable shot insights like carry distance, total distance, club head speed, and ball speed on every single shot.

Toptracer technology offers tons of data and all kinds of games to engage your imagination, your swing, your friends, and even new opponents around the world. From the classic Longest Drive challenge to virtually playing some of the world's most famous golf courses.

Toptracer is introducing golf to a new generation of players and bringing people together in meaningful ways. We're excited to share that experience with you!

The Toptracer Range App

The Toptracer Range app is where the magic happens. When guests create a profile, they instantly become part of a global golf community. The app stores data from practice sessions so players can monitor changes and fine tune their swing over time.

For users at Toptracer Range Mobile-enabled facilities, the app is also where they can access skill-based games that will drive their range session.

Interactive Practice

For golfers, Toptracer Range is the ultimate practice tool, taking the guesswork out of range sessions by offering an engaging, data-driven experience that appeals to everyone.

For groups of friends, Toptracer allows everyone to have fun, whether they are a seasoned single-digit handicapper or a complete beginner. The variety of interactive multiplayer games can be enjoyed by everyone together!

Toptracer has several game modes ranging from Long Drive, Closest to the Pin, Go Fish (for kids), as well as Practice Mode, Warm-Up Mode, and much more.

Interactive Experiences

Explore All Game Modes

Toptracer features a diverse collection of game configurations suitable for serious practice and group entertainment.

Closest to the Pin

Test your wedge control on legendary par-3 greens. Watch shot trajectories trace live on screen to see who gets nearest the cup.

Longest Drive

Unleash the big stick! Compete against friends in your stall or climb local range boards by hitting absolute bombs.

Go Fish

A family-friendly favorite. Catch virtual sea creatures by hitting shots into target rings. Collect badges and unlock trophies!

Virtual Golf

Play full 18-hole rounds on legendary PGA Tour layouts like Pebble Beach, Torrey Pines, and St Andrews right from Marsh Road.

Scramble Golf

Team up in groups to compete together. Play the best shot of each turn in a relaxed team scramble, perfect for casual parties.

Points Game

Aim for high-scoring rings out on the range field. Earn more points by landing shots further away and closer to targets.

My Practice

The ultimate training board. Evaluate consistency matrices, check individual club dispersion charts, and map your bag.

Approach Challenge

Test iron and wedge consistency by hitting approaches on various iconic course holes. Earn rankings on execution accuracy.

Warm Up

A simple, quick-start configuration designed to capture shot data during initial swings before getting into deeper game modes.
