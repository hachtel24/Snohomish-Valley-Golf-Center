Championship 18-Hole Course

Championship Putting Layout

Winding through beautifully landscaped rock-lined ponds, flowing streams, and rustic wooden bridges, our 18-hole course offers a premium putting test for players of all ages and skills.

Designed to mirror natural course contours, it features real artificial turf greens, sand hazards, and elevation changes that require deliberate touch. Perfect for friendly tournaments, family outings, and twilight matches under the overhead string lights.

The Practice Putting Green

Fine-Tune Your Touch

Improve your short game on our beautifully manicured, premium grass putting green. Perfect for pre-round warmups or dedicated putting drills.

Practice Green Etiquette & Rules

  • • Admission: Free to use for range bucket purchasers and mini-golf players.
  • • Hours: Open daily from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
  • • Putting vs Chipping: Chipping is strictly confined to the designated chipping bunker and green to protect the main putting turf.
  • • Equipment: Please bring your own practice golf balls. Range balls are not allowed on the putting green.

Need Help with Your Scoring?
