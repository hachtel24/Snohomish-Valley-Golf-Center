Daily Closing Schedule

To help you plan your evening practice or family rounds, please note our nightly step-down policy:

  • ▸ 9:30 PM: Beer, wine, cider, seltzers, and bucket sales end.
  • ▸ 10:00 PM: Lights turn off, gate closes, alarm set.
