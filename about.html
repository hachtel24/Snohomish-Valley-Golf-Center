Five Generations in the Valley

Our Story

Family-owned, community-focused. The Stocker family welcomes you to Snohomish Valley Golf Center, a gathering place built on generations of valley heritage.

The Stocker Family Heritage

Our family has lived and farmed in the Snohomish River Valley for five generations, loving this land and this close-knit community.

Family Owned & Operated by

  • Tim & Tracy Stocker
  • TJ & Mandy Stocker
  • Stubby & Lisa Stocker

At Snohomish Valley Golf Center, our goal is simple: we want every single guest to have so much fun during each visit that they cannot wait to return. We commit to continuous, ongoing facilities improvements to ensure your range, dining, and putting experience remains premium and welcoming all year round.

Location & Contact

Nestled beautifully in the Snohomish River Valley, our facility is easily accessible from Snohomish, Everett, and Woodinville. Stop by, hit a bucket of balls, grab a bite, and stay for the sunset.
